Custom Booking Platforms for Spas & Clinics

Specialized booking platforms for spas, clinics, and wellness centers. Appointment scheduling, client management, automated confirmations, and revenue protection built for healthcare businesses.

Platform capabilities

Scheduling

Intelligent appointment scheduling

Smart booking engine that handles complex scheduling requirements — multiple practitioners, service durations, room assignments, and buffer times between appointments.

Client Management

Complete client profiles

Unified client records with appointment history, preferences, treatment notes, and communication logs. Everything your front desk and practitioners need in one place.

Confirmations

Automated confirmation & reminders

Multi-channel automated reminders via SMS, email, and voice calls. Reduce no-shows by up to 67% with intelligent confirmation sequences powered by Droz Connect.

Revenue

Revenue protection & recovery

Deposit collection, cancellation policies, and waitlist management that protect your revenue. Automatically fill cancelled slots from your waitlist to maximize utilization.
