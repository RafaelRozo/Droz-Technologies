Our Services

Turbo Generator Expertise

We provide specialized alignment and balancing services for the most critical rotating equipment in power generation.

Alignment

Precision Turbo Generator Alignment

Laser-based alignment of turbo generator sets to manufacturer specifications. We ensure optimal alignment under both cold and hot operating conditions, accounting for thermal growth patterns.

Balancing

Multi-Plane Rotor Balancing

High-speed balancing of generator rotors, turbine rotors, and exciter assemblies. Field and shop balancing to ISO 1940 standards with detailed documentation.

Diagnostics

Vibration Diagnostics & Monitoring

Comprehensive vibration analysis of turbo generator trains including bearing assessment, rotor dynamics evaluation, and continuous monitoring program setup.

Inspection

Bearing & Exciter Inspection

Detailed inspection of journal bearings, thrust bearings, and exciter systems. We identify wear patterns, clearance issues, and potential failure modes.

Our team has decades of hands-on experience with turbo generators across thermal, hydroelectric, and combined-cycle power plants. We've worked alongside partners like Westinghouse to maintain the reliability of critical power infrastructure.
