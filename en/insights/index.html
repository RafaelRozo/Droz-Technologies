§ Editorial workflow

How a post gets here.

Insights is a curated wall, not a feed. Four steps stand between an idea and a published post — in the open, on the record.

  1. 01

    Draft the plan

    An engineer or partner proposes a topic and writes the page plan — audience, source engagement, sections, citations. Drafts sit in the planning folder until they are coherent.

  2. 02

    Plan review

    A senior partner reviews the plan for accuracy, sourcing, and commercial alignment. Where a client is referenced, sign-off is secured before the writer-agent is dispatched.

  3. 03

    Writing

    Senior writers turn the plan into a page. The output is a single editorial document, not a stream of fragments — everything sourced, everything tied back to work shipped.

  4. 04

    Index update

    The editor adds the new card to the Latest grid and, where merited, swaps the Featured card. Subscribers on matching topics receive the post within 24 hours.
