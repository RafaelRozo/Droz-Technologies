§ From the field

The disciplines that keep plants running.

Four diagnostic practices run inside every Droz reliability program — captured in the field on rotating equipment, motors, and refining assets across North America and Latin America.

DZ · 01
Vibration Analysis
Spectral diagnostics on rotating equipment

Bearing wear, imbalance, misalignment, and looseness identified from signature analysis on motors, pumps, and compressors.
DZ · 02
Thermography
Infrared inspection of plant systems

Switchgear, MCCs, transformers, and mechanical drive trains screened for thermal anomalies under operating load.
DZ · 03
Ultrasound
Acoustic detection of early-stage faults

Lubrication regime, leak detection, electrical partial discharge, and bearing pre-failure regimes captured before vibration onset.
DZ · 04
Laser Alignment
Precision shaft & coupling alignment

Coupled rotating equipment aligned to OEM tolerance — reducing seal failure, coupling wear, and vibration-driven downtime.
