§ Who we hire

Four personas. One hiring bar.

§ Benefits & culture

What it is like to work here.

Senior partners lead engagements

Every engagement has a senior partner accountable end-to-end. You learn from people who have done the work for two decades, not from a slide deck.

No offshoring of judgment

We do not subcontract the work we are paid for. The person you meet on the kickoff call is the person who delivers.

Twenty-plus years of stability

Founded 2004. Profitable. No outside capital. We hire when we have work for you, and we keep you for the long arc.

EN / FR / ES collaboration

Burlington, Toronto, Houston, Mexico City. You work across three languages and three regulatory regimes. We support you with translation, travel, and clear written norms.

Burlington HQ + remote

Burlington is the centre of gravity; we host quarterly engineering weeks on site. Most roles are remote-first within Canada / US / MX; field roles travel to client sites.

§ How we hire

Five steps. On the record.

The hiring process is published here so the firm is accountable to its own description. Every candidate moves through these five steps in this order.

  1. 01

    Application

    Submit your résumé and a short cover note via the role page. We read every application within a week.

  2. 02

    Screening

    A 30-minute conversation with a hiring partner to understand your trajectory and confirm fit on the role’s core requirements.

  3. 03

    Practice interview

    A working session with one of the senior practitioners on the team you would join. Talk through a recent engagement of theirs and a recent project of yours. No whiteboarding theatre.

  4. 04

    Technical interview

    A scenario rooted in real work. For engineering roles, a vibration / thermography case study. For software roles, a system-design walkthrough on a real Droz problem. We share the brief 48 hours ahead.

  5. 05

    Offer

    A written offer within one week of the final interview. We move quickly once we are aligned; we expect the same back.

§ Privacy

Applications are handled under the Ontario privacy regime and the firm’s Privacy policy. Résumés and cover letters are stored for 12 months unless you ask for earlier deletion.
