§ Why a portfolio
One culture. Eight problems solved.

Droz Technologies treats software products as long-lived assets, not seasonal launches. Each platform ships under its own brand because each one solves a different operational problem for a different buyer — but every platform shares one engineering culture, one security posture, and one shared cloud and AI stack. Built in Burlington · shipped across CA · US · MX.

§ Engagement model

How a Droz product engagement begins.

01 · Scoping
01
Scoping conversation

A senior partner — not a sales rep — walks the prospect through the platform that fits the operational problem.

02 · Pilot
02
Pilot or proof

Every product engagement begins with a defined pilot: scope, success criteria, and timeline are written down before contracts.

03 · Production
03
Production deployment

The pilot graduates to production with a named technical owner, SLAs, and a 90-day reliability review.

04 · Partnership
04
Ongoing partnership

Droz stays inside the engagement — product roadmap calls, quarterly reviews, and direct access to the engineers who built the platform.

§ Built on
