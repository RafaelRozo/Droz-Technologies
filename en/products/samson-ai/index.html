Samson AI: multi-platform paid acquisition, on autopilot.

Samson AI is a standalone marketing automation platform that runs and scales paid acquisition across every major ad surface — finding what performs best for a brand, then automating the budget shift toward it. Built for performance marketers who'd rather oversee an AI than tune ad sets manually.

§ What it is

One operator UI. Every major ad surface.

Samson AI is a marketing automation platform built for one job: find what's working in a brand's paid-acquisition mix, automate the budget shift toward it, and keep the brand safely inside its own guardrails while doing so. Samson lives outside any single ad platform — it runs across Meta, Google, TikTok, LinkedIn, X, YouTube, and DSPs from one operator UI.

  • Cross-platform creative testing at scale. Generate, deploy, and measure creative across every channel from one interface.
  • Budget reallocation toward winners automatically. Samson moves spend toward the creative-channel pairs that are converting, on the cadence the team configures.
  • Brand-safe scaling with human approval gates. Every change above a threshold is reviewable; nothing the team didn't authorize goes live.
§ Who it's for

Three buyer profiles. One platform.

Persona 01

Performance marketing
at D2C brands

Running multi-channel paid acquisition. Tired of the Friday-afternoon spreadsheet of platform performance. Want an AI that handles the platform-by-platform optimization so the team can focus on creative direction and brand.

Persona 02

Growth leaders
at scaleups

Scaling paid acquisition to support a revenue plan. Need predictable, automated channel mix optimization without hiring a paid acquisition manager per channel.

Persona 03

Agencies running
multi-client portfolios

Managing a portfolio of brands across the same set of ad platforms. Samson gives them per-client visibility, per-client guardrails, and an order-of-magnitude operating leverage on their paid-media operations team.

§ Capabilities

Six surfaces, one operator UI.

01 · Creative

Creative generation

Image, copy, and video script generation tuned to each platform's specs and the brand's voice.

02 · Deploy

Cross-platform deployment

Publish to Meta, Google, TikTok, LinkedIn, X, YouTube, and DSPs from one interface; per-platform optimization is automatic.

03 · Measure

Performance & attribution

Pulls performance data from every connected platform; reconciles into one cross-platform view with attribution modelling.

04 · Reallocate

Automated budget reallocation

Samson shifts spend toward winning creative-channel pairs on the team's configured cadence — daily, hourly, or under explicit approval.

05 · Audience

Audience modeling & lookalikes

Builds audiences from CDP / CRM signals and pushes lookalikes into each ad platform.

06 · Brand safety

Compliance & brand-safety guardrails

Per-brand rules: no claim language outside the approved list, no placement on excluded sites, no creative below quality threshold.
