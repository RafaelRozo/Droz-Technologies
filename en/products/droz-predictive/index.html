§ What it is

Failures ranked by lead time, criticality, and cost.

Droz Predictive is a predictive maintenance platform for industrial operators. It ingests vibration spectra, thermography frames, ultrasound captures, oil-analysis results, and CMMS work-orders, then surfaces the failures that haven't happened yet — ranked by lead time, by criticality, and by the cost of being wrong.

  • Multi-discipline by default. Vibration, thermography, ultrasound, and oil analysis live in one asset record, not four.
  • AI-driven, but auditable. Every alert ships with the residual, the spectral fingerprint, and the analyst notes that produced it.
  • Operator-grade. Built for plants that already have a reliability team and want a platform that respects how they already work.

§ Capabilities

One asset record. Every discipline.

01 · Registry

Asset registry

Every motor, pump, fan, gearbox, and compressor lives in one hierarchical asset tree with criticality, RUL, and last-inspection state.

02 · Vibration

Vibration intake

Time-waveform + FFT spectra from Baker, Easy-Laser, IRD, Prüftechnik. Automatic alarm bands per ISO 10816.

03 · Thermography

Thermography intake

FLIR / Panametrics images and reports, geo-tagged to the asset record, annotated by the analyst.

04 · AI

AI residual engine

Spectral residuals, trend deviation, and pattern-match against the Droz asset-intelligence library.

05 · Workflow

Work-order routing

Findings push to the customer's CMMS (SAP PM, Maximo, Fiix, eMaint) with severity, recommended action, and lead time.

06 · Reports

Reliability reports

Monthly site reports, quarterly corporate rollups, and audit-ready records for ISO 9001 and ISO 55000 programs.
