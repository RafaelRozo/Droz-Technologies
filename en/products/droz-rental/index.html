Droz Rental brings reservations, fleet utilization, damage tracking, pricing, and the customer portal into one operator-grade platform — built for rental car chains, corporate fleets, and peer-to-peer marketplaces that treat their vehicles as a portfolio, not a list of line items in a spreadsheet.

§ What it is

One operator UI. The whole fleet.

Droz Rental is a vehicle and fleet rental operations platform. One asset record per vehicle. One reservation engine. One pricing surface. One damage and telematics view. One customer portal. Replaces the spreadsheet-plus-folder-of-photos workflow with an auditable operator UI that the entire team — counter staff, fleet ops, maintenance, finance — runs the same way every day.

  • Vehicle-level asset record. Every vehicle has one record with VIN, status, location, telematics state, damage history, maintenance schedule, and reservation log.
  • Fleet-level portfolio view. Utilization, downtime, per-vehicle revenue, per-class profitability — the fleet treated as a portfolio.
  • Customer surface built in. Branded reservation, online check-in, damage walk-around capture, and post-rental settlement — the same data trail end-to-end.
§ Who it's for

Three buyer profiles. One platform.

Persona 01

Rental operator
independent or chain

Runs 50–5,000 vehicles across multiple locations. Wants one platform the entire team runs on — reservations, counter, fleet ops, damage, finance — without bolting four systems together.

Persona 02

Corporate fleet
manager

Runs a fleet of pool vehicles for an enterprise's staff or field teams. Wants visibility into utilization, downtime, and total cost per vehicle without paying for a full rental-chain stack.

Persona 03

P2P rental
marketplace

Two-sided marketplace where the supply is private vehicle owners. Wants the operator backbone — verification, damage workflow, claims — without rebuilding it from scratch.

§ Capabilities

Six surfaces. One operator UI.

01 · Reserve

Reservation engine

Class-based and unit-specific reservations with availability, deposits, and modifications.

02 · Utilization

Fleet utilization analytics

Per-vehicle, per-class, per-location utilization, downtime, and revenue.

03 · Damage

Damage & telematics

Pre / post inspection capture, photo evidence, telematics-driven incident detection, and damage-claim workflow.

04 · Pricing

Pricing optimization

Dynamic pricing by demand signal, season, location, and competitive set — operator-tunable.

05 · Customer

Customer portal

Branded reservation, online check-in, walk-around capture, payment, and post-rental settlement.

06 · Loyalty

Loyalty & repeat-customer flows

Profile, history, referral, and segmented offers — without bolting on a separate loyalty platform.
