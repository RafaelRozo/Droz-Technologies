§ What it is

Institutional rigor. Boutique cadence.

Droz Investments is an AI + quant portfolio platform. Construction, rebalancing, multi-asset reporting, alt-data integration, AI-assisted research, risk monitoring, and a regulator-ready audit trail — all in one operator UI. Built for the tier of advisor, wealth firm, and family office that wants institutional infrastructure without institutional procurement cost, and used internally by Droz Technologies to run its own investment positions.

  • Quant + AI construction. Portfolio construction grounded in quantitative models, augmented by AI for research synthesis and counterargument surfacing.
  • Regulator-ready by default. IIROC / OSC / AMF / FINRA-aligned audit trail, trade capture, and reporting baked into the workflow.
  • Built and battle-tested by Droz. The same platform Droz uses for its own positions — the customer is buying infrastructure that's already operating with real capital behind it.

Persona 02

wealth firm

$100M–$5B AUM. Operates between the high-touch family office and the mid-tier asset manager. Needs one platform across portfolio, compliance, reporting, and client communication.

Persona 03

Family
office

Multi-asset, multi-jurisdiction. Long horizons. Needs a platform that handles alternative assets, alt-data integration, and the auditable trail a family principal expects.

§ Capabilities

Six surfaces. One operator UI.

01 · Construction

Portfolio construction

Quant signals informed by AI research synthesis; allocation against a model with explainable rationale.

02 · Rebalance

Rebalancing

Drift-aware rebalancing with tax-lot awareness and trading-cost modelling.

03 · Report

Multi-asset reporting

Time-weighted return, attribution, and client-ready reports across equities, fixed income, alternatives, and cash.

04 · Alt-data

Alt-data integration

Ingest alternative-data feeds and surface the signals that matter alongside the model output.

05 · Research

AI-assisted research

Long-form research synthesis with citations; counterargument surfacing; per-holding thesis tracking.

06 · Audit

Regulator-ready audit trail

Trade capture, compliance restriction checks, and exportable record packages for IIROC / OSC / AMF / FINRA.

§ Where it ships

Advisors, firms, family offices — and Droz itself.

Droz Investments ships to independent registered investment advisors, boutique wealth firms, and family offices in Canada and the United States, with planned LATAM expansion. The platform is also used internally by Droz Technologies to manage its own investment positions across the network.
