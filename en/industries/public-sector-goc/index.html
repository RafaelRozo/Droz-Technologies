Public sector: a Government of Canada supplier.

Droz Technologies is registered as a Government of Canada supplier and delivers reliability engineering, applied AI, and enterprise software to federal departments and crown corporations. Engagements honor SOC 2 Type II information-security controls, ISO 9001 quality management, and Protected-B information handling.

§ Procurement context

Procurement readiness.

Droz is structured to respond to federal procurement instruments — standing offers, supply arrangements, and one-off solicitations — without retooling between engagements.

  • Supplier identity. Registered Government of Canada supplier; Procurement Business Number on file.
  • Information security. SOC 2 Type II controls; documented Protected-B handling procedures for personnel and data.
  • Quality system. ISO 9001 quality management with documented engagement procedures.
  • Bilingual delivery. EN / FR-CA on every engagement; ES for trilingual program work.
