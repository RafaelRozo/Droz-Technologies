§ Member · 01

Rockwell Automation

Industrial automation hardware and software — including Allen-Bradley PLCs, ControlLogix / CompactLogix controllers, FactoryTalk SCADA, and the Rockwell ecosystem broadly used across North American manufacturing.

How Droz applies it

Droz integrates with existing Rockwell control stacks for data extraction, historian tie-ins, and OT-to-cloud bridges feeding reliability dashboards. We do not perform control-system engineering of record; our work sits adjacent to the OT layer and reads from it under change-controlled access.

Where it shows up

  • Modern IT Infrastructure
  • Business Process Automation
  • Reliability Data & Monitoring
  • General Manufacturing
  • Automotive
§ Member · 02

FLIR Systems

Industrial thermal imaging cameras and the analysis software that turns radiometric images into condition assessments — the dominant family of equipment in North American electrical and mechanical thermography programmes.

How Droz applies it

Droz thermography surveys (electrical switchgear, MCCs, transformers, mechanical bearings, refractories, building envelopes) are performed with FLIR-class radiometric cameras at the CINDE-defined Infrared Thermography certification level appropriate to the asset.

Where it shows up

  • Thermography
  • Predictive Maintenance Services
  • Power Generation
  • Refineries · Oil & Gas
  • Cement Plants
§ Member · 03

Panametrics

Ultrasonic measurement and inspection equipment — thickness gauges, flow meters, and ultrasonic flaw detectors used in pressure-vessel and pipework integrity programmes. Now part of Baker Hughes; the Panametrics brand persists for the instrument line.

How Droz applies it

Droz uses Panametrics-class ultrasonic thickness and flow instruments for piping and vessel surveys in refineries, petrochemicals, and process plants. Records cite the gauge family and calibration block traceability.

Where it shows up

  • Ultrasound
  • Predictive Maintenance Services
  • Refineries · Oil & Gas
  • Petrochemicals
  • Industrial Chemicals
§ Member · 04

Baker Instrument

Static and dynamic motor analyzers — surge testing, polarization index, megohmmeter and PdMA-style off-line and on-line motor diagnostics — used to characterize stator insulation, rotor cage, air-gap eccentricity, and connection integrity. Now part of Megger; the Baker line persists.

How Droz applies it

Droz electrical motor testing engagements use Baker-class instruments to baseline and trend critical motors; findings cross-reference EASA AR100 expectations for any repair-route decisions.

Where it shows up

  • Vibration Analysis
  • Predictive Maintenance Services
  • General Manufacturing
  • Mining & Metals
  • Pulp & Paper
§ Member · 05

Easy-Laser

Laser shaft alignment, geometric measurement, and machinery setup tools — the Swedish-origin family of instruments widely used for coupled-machine alignment, soft-foot diagnostics, and base-plate flatness.

How Droz applies it

Droz performs precision laser shaft alignment for couplings, belt-driven trains, and base-plate corrections using Easy-Laser-class equipment. Pre-and-post alignment reports record the residual offset and angularity against tolerance.

Where it shows up

  • Laser Alignment
  • Predictive Maintenance Services
  • General Manufacturing
  • Automotive
  • Food Manufacturing
§ Member · 06

IRD Balancing

Dynamic balancing instrumentation and methodology — single-plane, two-plane, and multi-plane balancing of rotors, fans, blowers, and pumps in situ or on a balancing machine. One of the longest-standing names in the discipline.

How Droz applies it

Droz performs field balancing on critical rotating equipment using IRD-class instrumentation and the ISO 1940 balance-quality grades appropriate to the machine class. Residual unbalance is documented with the balance grade achieved.

Where it shows up

  • Vibration Analysis
  • Predictive Maintenance Services
  • General Manufacturing
  • Pulp & Paper
  • Cement Plants
§ Member · 07

Krautkramer

Ultrasonic flaw detectors and NDT instrumentation — the German-origin family of UT equipment widely used for weld inspection, casting integrity, and component flaw detection. Now part of Baker Hughes Waygate Technologies; the Krautkramer brand persists for the instrument line.

How Droz applies it

Droz NDT engagements use Krautkramer-class flaw detectors for weld and casting inspection, performed at CINDE-defined UT certification levels with calibration block traceability documented in every report.

Where it shows up

  • Ultrasound
  • Predictive Maintenance Services
  • Refineries · Oil & Gas
  • Petrochemicals
  • Nuclear Power
§ Member · 08

Prüftechnik

Laser alignment and condition-monitoring instruments — including the OPTALIGN / ROTALIGN shaft-alignment family and VIBXPERT vibration analyzers. German-origin; now part of Fluke Reliability.

How Droz applies it

Where plants already operate Prüftechnik OPTALIGN / ROTALIGN tooling and VIBXPERT collectors, Droz engineers work natively in those workflows, export data into our reliability platform without conversion friction, and support site teams to interpret the results against the relevant ISO 10816 / 20816 severity bands.

Where it shows up

  • Laser Alignment
  • Vibration Analysis
  • Predictive Maintenance Services
  • Reliability Data & Monitoring
