An ecosystem index, not a marketing roster. Each group is the floor for how a Droz engagement is built — the bodies whose standards we work to, the instruments our field teams operate, and the cloud and AI platforms our software runs on.

§ The operating logic
How Droz uses this ecosystem.

Four short sentences that explain the seam between the three groups and the work Droz does.

Group · 01

Engineers.

Field engineers are certified or trained against the frameworks listed in Group 01 · Associations. Reports cite the analyst category and the clause that grounds the finding.

Group · 02

Instruments.

The technologies in Group 02 are the instruments our teams carry; their raw data feeds our reliability platform without conversion friction.

Group · 03

Software.

Droz's products and platforms run on the cloud, AI, and orchestration partners in Group 03 — chosen per engagement, never per preference.
