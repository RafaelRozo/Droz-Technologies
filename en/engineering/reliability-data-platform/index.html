§ Definition
Condition history, work orders, KPIs —
under one corporate lens.

Most plants have the data. What they lack is a single, governed surface that makes the data comparable across sites and accountable to corporate engineering. Droz's reliability data platform pulls condition history, work-order activity, asset hierarchies, and KPI baselines from your existing systems — CMMS, SCADA, historians, ERP — into one normalized layer with clear ownership, retention, and lineage. Plant teams keep working in their tools. Corporate engineering gets one screen — bearing-failure trends across sites, top reactive-loss assets, MTBF/MTTR by family — without bespoke spreadsheets. The layer is the foundation pillar C's models train on, and the surface pillar A's field reports land in.

§ Data governance & security
Plant data is sensitive.
Treat it like it.

Reliability data is operational, often confidential, and increasingly a regulated asset. Droz's platform ships with governance, security, and compliance built in — not bolted on after deployment.

GOV · 01

Ownership
& lineage

Every metric, every dashboard, every alert has an owner, a definition, and a lineage trail back to its source system. KPI definitions are versioned; changes require sign-off.

Data dictionaryKPI versioningLineageOwner-of-record
GOV · 02

Security posture

SOC 2 Type II in production. Encryption at rest and in transit. Role-based access control with site-scoped permissions. Audit logs retained for the agreed retention window. Protected-B-aligned patterns available for public-sector workloads.

& residency

Data residency configurable per jurisdiction (Canada, US, EU, Mexico). Retention policies defined per data class — raw signals, derived metrics, work-order records — with hard-delete on schedule.
