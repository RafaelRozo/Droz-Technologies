§ Capabilities

What we automate.

01 · RPA

Robotic Process
Automation

Screen-scraping and UI-driven bots for legacy systems with no usable API. UiPath or Automation Anywhere. Quarantined runtime, audit log, and a deliberate retirement plan as the underlying system modernizes.

UiPath Automation Anywhere Audit log
02 · API Integrations

Direct system-to-system,
contract-tested

Direct system-to-system wiring. REST and GraphQL where they exist; webhook bridges where they don't. Contract-tested, idempotent, with retry and dead-letter handling.

REST GraphQL Webhooks
03 · Workflow Orchestration

Business processes
as code

Business processes modeled as code: branching, retries, human approval steps, SLAs, and a state machine you can replay. Temporal, Camunda, or Argo Workflows when the workload warrants.

Temporal Camunda Argo
04 · iPaaS

The platform you can
sustain matters most

Zapier, Make, n8n, or Tray.ai — picked per workload. The platform you can sustain matters more than the platform with the longest connector list.

Zapier Make n8n Tray.ai
§ Engagement

Discovery → Design → Build → Handover.

01 · Discovery
01
Discovery

One- to two-week walkthrough with your operations and IT leads. Output: a written process map, a backlog of automation candidates, and a ROI estimate per candidate.

02 · Design
02
Design

Per candidate: target state diagram, tool selection, integration points, exception handling, governance model. Reviewed and signed before any code.

03 · Build
03
Build

Phased delivery in 2-week sprints. Sandbox first, then dual-run with the existing manual process, then cutover with a rollback button.

04 · Handover
04
Handover

Documentation, runbooks, training for your operations and IT teams, and a 60-day stabilization. Engagement either closes or transitions to a managed-service contract.
