§ Engagement

Discovery → Design → Build → Handover.

01 · Discovery
01
Discovery

Senior partner runs the kickoff: user interviews, stakeholder mapping, hypothesis log. Output: a product brief + a journey map.

02 · Design
02
Design

Information architecture, wireframes, design system, and clickable prototypes in Figma. Reviewed weekly with your team; locked before sprint 1.

03 · Build
03
Build

2-week sprints. Web in Next.js or React; mobile in React Native or SwiftUI. CI pipeline ships to staging on every merge; production releases gated by a release-train cadence.

04 · Handover
04
Handover

App Store / Play Store submission, web hosting handover, monitoring dashboards, support runbook, and a 90-day stabilization. Transitions to a managed-service contract on day 91 if needed.
