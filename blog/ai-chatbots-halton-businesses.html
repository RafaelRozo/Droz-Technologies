How 24/7 Lead Capture Actually Works

Modern AI chatbots aren't the clunky decision-tree scripts you remember from 2018. They're powered by large language models that understand natural language, hold context across a conversation, and respond with the nuance of a well-trained team member.

Here's what a typical lead capture flow looks like for a business using our platform:

  1. Visitor arrives. The chatbot greets them with a context-aware message based on the page they're viewing. Someone on your "Emergency Plumbing" page gets a different opener than someone browsing "Kitchen Renovations."
  2. Qualification questions. The bot asks 2–3 targeted questions—service needed, timeline, location—mirroring exactly how your best salesperson opens a conversation.
  3. Instant answers. Pricing ranges, service areas, hours of operation, FAQs—the chatbot handles 85% of routine questions without human involvement.
  4. Lead capture. Name, email, phone number, and service details are collected conversationally—not through a cold, static form.
  5. CRM push & notification. The lead and full conversation transcript land in your CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho, or others) and your team gets a Slack or email alert in real time.

The result? Businesses we work with in the Halton Region see 3–5× more qualified leads from the same website traffic within the first 60 days. No ad spend increase. No SEO overhaul. Just capturing the demand that was already there—and slipping through the cracks.
