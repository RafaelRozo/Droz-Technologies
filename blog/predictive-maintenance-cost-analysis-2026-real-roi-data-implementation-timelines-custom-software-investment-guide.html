Decision Framework: When to Choose SaaS vs Custom

\1

  • Immediate deployment required (days/weeks, not months)
  • Standard workflows (CRM, email marketing, accounting functions)
  • Small team usage (under 20 users) with limited scaling plans
  • Testing new processes with uncertain long-term adoption
  • Annual budget under $20,000

The Hybrid Strategy for 2026

Leading companies combine both approaches: SaaS for commodity functions (email, accounting, project management) and custom software for core business differentiators (proprietary analytics, customer-facing platforms, AI-powered operational systems).
