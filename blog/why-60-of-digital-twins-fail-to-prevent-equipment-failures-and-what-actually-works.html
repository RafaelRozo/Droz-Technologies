The Industrial Reality: Why Physical Beats Digital

\1 Digital twins depend on perfect sensor data. In harsh industrial environments—high temperatures, vibration, electromagnetic interference—sensors fail, drift, and provide inconsistent data.

\1 85% of equipment failures occur outside digital twin model parameters. Real machinery behaves differently than theoretical models, especially after years of operation.

\1 Modern digital twin platforms require specialized data scientists, cloud infrastructure, and constant model tuning. Traditional monitoring needs experienced technicians—skills most plants already have.
