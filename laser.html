The Silent Killer of Rotating Equipment

Shaft misalignment is one of the leading causes of premature machinery failure, yet its effects are often misdiagnosed. Even minor deviations can place enormous stress on bearings, seals, and couplings.

Angular Misalignment

Occurs when shafts are not parallel, creating a destructive bending force on the coupling and adjacent bearings with every rotation.

Parallel Misalignment

Happens when shafts are parallel but not on the same axis, leading to excessive wear, vibration, and energy loss.
