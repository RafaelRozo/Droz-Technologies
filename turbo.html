The Unforgiving Environment of a Turbo Generator

A turbo generator is a symphony of extreme forces. High speeds, immense temperatures, and massive rotational mass create an environment where even the slightest imperfection can lead to catastrophic failure. Maintaining the precise alignment and balance of these multi-ton rotors is one of the most demanding tasks in industrial maintenance.

This is not a task for generalists. It requires specialized equipment, years of experience, and an unwavering commitment to precision.

Our Specialized Turbo Generator Services

We provide a complete solution for ensuring the mechanical integrity of your turbine and generator train.

Precision Alignment

Using advanced laser trackers and optical alignment systems, we perform comprehensive alignment checks on the entire turbine-generator train. This includes bearing journal positioning, casing alignment, and diaphragm alignment, accounting for thermal growth to ensure perfect alignment under operating conditions.

High-Speed Dynamic Balancing

We perform multi-plane, high-speed dynamic balancing of turbo generator rotors to ISO standards. This process corrects for the minute imbalances that cause destructive vibrations at operational speeds, protecting bearings, seals, and the overall structural integrity of the machine.

The Cost of Imperfection

In the context of a turbo generator, minor issues cascade into major failures with staggering financial consequences.

Forced Outages

Unplanned downtime of a major power generation asset can result in revenue losses measured in the hundreds of thousands of dollars per day.

Catastrophic Damage

An unbalanced or misaligned rotor can lead to bearing wipes, blade rubs, seal failures, and in the worst cases, a thrown blade or complete rotor destruction.

Reduced Efficiency

Even minor issues increase vibration and friction, leading to a loss of efficiency that translates to significant fuel waste over time.
