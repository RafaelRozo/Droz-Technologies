What we do

Vibration Analysis

Condition monitoring that catches failures early

Multi-channel vibration data collection and analysis on pumps, motors, fans, compressors, and turbines. We identify bearing defects, imbalance, misalignment, and structural resonance before they cause unplanned downtime.
Laser Alignment

Precision shaft alignment

Laser-based alignment for coupled rotating machinery. Eliminates premature bearing and seal failures caused by misalignment — one of the most common root causes of equipment breakdown in industrial settings.
Dynamic Balancing

In-situ and shop balancing

Single and multi-plane dynamic balancing for fans, blowers, rotors, and impellers. Field balancing without disassembly saves days of downtime and eliminates shipping costs for heavy rotating components.
Ultrasound Testing

Airborne and structural ultrasound inspection

Leak detection, bearing assessment, and electrical inspection using ultrasonic technology. Detects compressed air leaks, steam traps, and early-stage bearing degradation that vibration analysis alone may miss.
Turbo Generators

Turbine and generator services

Diagnostics, repair, and maintenance for turbo generator sets in power generation. Includes rotor balancing, bearing inspection, exciter maintenance, and operational monitoring programs.
Reliability Programs

From reactive to predictive

Complete reliability program design — route-based monitoring schedules, KPI frameworks, alarm threshold configuration, and integration with plant CMMS systems. We build the program, train your team, and hand it off.
