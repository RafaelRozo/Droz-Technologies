Hearing What Your Machines Can't Say.

Our advanced ultrasound analysis services detect high-frequency sounds of friction, turbulence, and electrical faults that are inaudible to the human ear, allowing us to pinpoint costly failures before they happen.

What We Detect

Uncovering Invisible Threats to Your Operation

Ultrasound technology is a versatile, non-destructive tool that excels at identifying a wide range of mechanical and electrical issues across your facility.

Compressed Air & Gas Leaks

Leaks are a significant source of wasted energy. We can pinpoint the exact location of even the smallest leaks in noisy environments, saving you thousands in energy costs.

Bearing Faults & Lubrication Issues

Before a bearing begins to vibrate or overheat, it generates ultrasonic friction. We detect these early warning signs, allowing for proactive lubrication or replacement.

Steam Trap Failures

A single failed steam trap can cost thousands per year in wasted steam. Our ultrasound analysis quickly identifies faulty traps that are open, closed, or leaking.

Electrical Faults

Detecting arcing, tracking, and corona in electrical equipment is critical for safety. Ultrasound provides a safe, early warning of potentially dangerous electrical failures.

Valve Leakage

We can assess the condition of internal valves, identifying leaks that affect product quality and operational efficiency without the need for a full shutdown.

Pump Cavitation

The destructive force of cavitation can be identified by its unique ultrasonic signature, allowing for corrective action before significant impeller damage occurs.

The Clear ROI of Ultrasound Analysis

Implementing an ultrasound program delivers immediate and long-term financial and operational benefits.

Massive Energy Savings

Identifying and fixing a single compressed air leak can save thousands of dollars per year. A facility-wide scan often reveals savings that pay for the service many times over.

Enhanced Plant Safety

Proactively identifying electrical faults and preventing catastrophic mechanical failures is crucial for protecting your personnel and assets.

Reduced Downtime

Ultrasound provides the earliest warning of developing issues, giving you maximum time to plan and schedule maintenance before a failure occurs.

Optimized Lubrication Schedules

Stop lubricating based on a calendar. We help you lubricate based on condition, preventing both under-lubrication and over-lubrication failures.

Our Technology & Process

Our certified technicians use best-in-class digital ultrasound detectors. These instruments listen for high-frequency sounds and translate them into audible signals and measurable data, allowing us to not only detect a fault but also assess its severity.

  1. **Facility Walk-Through:** We work with your team to identify critical assets and survey routes.
  2. **Data Collection:** Our technicians perform a systematic scan of your equipment.
  3. **Analysis & Diagnosis:** We analyze the collected data to identify and prioritize faults.
  4. **Actionable Reporting:** You receive a clear, detailed report with the exact location of faults and recommended corrective actions.
