Hearing the Invisible
Sound of Failure

Our advanced ultrasound analysis detects high-frequency sounds inaudible to the human ear, revealing steam trap failures, bearing defects, compressed air leaks, and electrical anomalies before they become costly problems.

Discover Applications

What Ultrasound Reveals

Ultrasonic technology operates beyond the range of human hearing, detecting the high-frequency sounds generated by turbulence, friction, and electrical discharge.

Compressed Air Leak Detection

Even small air leaks create ultrasonic turbulence. We pinpoint leak locations in noisy industrial environments, helping reduce energy costs and improve system pressure.

Steam Trap Testing

Failed steam traps waste massive amounts of energy. Ultrasound analysis distinguishes between properly functioning traps and those that are leaking or blocked.

Bearing Condition Assessment

Bearing defects generate unique ultrasonic signatures. We can detect early-stage bearing degradation that vibration analysis alone might miss.

Electrical Inspection

Electrical arcing, corona discharge, and tracking create distinctive ultrasonic emissions, allowing us to identify electrical faults before they cause equipment damage.

Valve & Pump Analysis

Internal valve leakage and cavitation in pumps produce characteristic ultrasonic patterns, enabling precise diagnosis of flow-related problems.

Lubrication Monitoring

Over-lubrication and under-lubrication both affect ultrasonic levels. We guide optimal lubrication practices to extend equipment life without waste.
