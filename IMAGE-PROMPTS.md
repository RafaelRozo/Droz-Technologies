### Post-Processing Notes
- Desaturate 15-20% from default
- Warm the white balance slightly (+5-10 on orange/yellow)
- Add subtle film grain (2-5%)
- Ensure no pure whites or pure blacks ‚Äî keep within the warm palette
- Export at 80% quality WebP for fast loading
- Generate @2x versions for retina

### Color Grading Reference
- Shadows: Push toward warm brown (#3a3028)
- Midtones: Neutral with slight warmth
- Highlights: Cream (#faf9f6), never pure white
- Overall: Think "shot on Portra 400 film"
