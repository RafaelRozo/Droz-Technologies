1. Introduction & Scope

Droz Technologies Inc. ("Droz Technologies," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website droztechnologies.com, use our products and services, or interact with us in any way.

This policy applies to all services offered by Droz Technologies, including but not limited to:

  • Digital Marketing & Growth Systems — marketing automation, SEO, paid media management
  • Predictive Maintenance & Engineering Solutions — industrial monitoring, vibration analysis, condition-based maintenance platforms
  • Custom Software & AI Solutions — web/mobile applications, AI automation, API integrations
  • Samson-AI — AI-powered advertising automation platform
  • Droz Connect — revenue protection platform for service businesses

By accessing or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our products and services, including AI-powered advertising, predictive maintenance platforms, and custom software solutions
  • Communication: To send service updates, technical notices, security alerts, and respond to your inquiries
  • Marketing: To provide personalized content, promotional offers, and educational resources (with your consent where required)
  • Analytics: To understand how our services are used, identify trends, and optimize performance
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, unauthorized access, and other illegal activities
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, and government requests
  • AI/ML Training: To improve our AI models and algorithms. Personal data used for training is anonymized and aggregated. You may opt out of AI training at any time.
  • Predictive Maintenance: To analyze equipment data and generate predictive insights for industrial clients. All industrial data is treated as confidential business information.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: Where you have given clear consent for us to process your personal data for a specific purpose
  • Contract: Where processing is necessary for the performance of a contract with you
  • Legitimate Interest: Where processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests, provided these are not overridden by your rights
  • Legal Obligation: Where processing is necessary to comply with a legal obligation
  • Vital Interest: Where processing is necessary to protect someone's life (applicable to industrial safety contexts)

7.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Access Control: Role-based access control (RBAC), multi-factor authentication (MFA), principle of least privilege
  • Network Security: Enterprise firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems (IDS/IPS), DDoS protection
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring, real-time alerting, automated threat detection
  • Vulnerability Management: Regular penetration testing, automated vulnerability scanning, responsible disclosure program
  • Backup & Recovery: Encrypted backups with geographically distributed redundancy, tested disaster recovery procedures

7.2 Organizational Safeguards

  • Employee background checks and security awareness training
  • Confidentiality and non-disclosure agreements for all personnel
  • Incident response plan with defined escalation procedures
  • Regular third-party security audits and assessments
  • Data Protection Officer (DPO) oversight
  • Privacy Impact Assessments (PIAs) for new products and features

7.3 HIPAA-Specific Safeguards

For clients in the healthcare sector, we implement additional protections:

  • Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) executed with all covered entities
  • Protected Health Information (PHI) encrypted with AES-256-GCM
  • Access logs maintained for minimum 6 years
  • Workforce training on HIPAA requirements
  • Breach notification within 60 days as required by the Breach Notification Rule
  • Physical safeguards for any systems processing PHI

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the following rights:

8.1 All Users

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previously given consent at any time
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of marketing communications at any time

8.2 GDPR (EU/EEA Residents)

  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to not be subject to automated decision-making
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

8.3 CCPA/CPRA (California Residents)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold
  • Right to delete personal information held by businesses
  • Right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal information
  • Right to limit use and disclosure of sensitive personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

8.4 PIPEDA (Canadian Residents)

  • Right to access your personal information held by the organization
  • Right to challenge the accuracy and completeness of your information
  • Right to withdraw consent (subject to legal or contractual restrictions)
  • Right to complain to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@droztechnologies.com. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days (or within the timeframe required by applicable law).
